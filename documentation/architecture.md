# Autonomous Quantitative Enterprise Architecture

## 1. Multi-Agent Ecosystem (`ai/`)
Deploys `AgentManager` controlling swarms estimating allocations and subjective textual sentiments.

## 2. Institutional Risk Vectors (`governance/`)
Orders routing to `MultiBroker/` arrays execute through restrictive `ComplianceEngine` checks guaranteeing no monolithic trade slices market limit-books dangerously.

## 3. High Availability (`observability/ & backup/`)
Orchestrates clustered node telemetry into robust `StateReplicas` ensuring local failure gracefully shifts weight to `BackupManagers`.
